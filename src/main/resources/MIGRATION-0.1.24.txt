VibePetCore 0.1.24

- Evolution GUI was expanded:
  - current pet menu now shows exact evolution requirements
  - per-pet help pages show every evolution step
  - player can click a pet card in GUI to open its detailed page
  - GUI shows which requirements are ready and which are missing

- Evolution items are now checked in two places:
  - player inventory
  - active pet backpack

- Evolution consumes the item from player inventory first, then from the pet backpack.

- Feeding XP is capped:
  - one food/rare-resource feeding can add at most 20% of the current level progress
  - the cap is applied after growth boost, so boosts cannot bypass it

- Real evolution readiness now matches the UI:
  - level requirement
  - readiness 10/10
  - one valid evolution item
