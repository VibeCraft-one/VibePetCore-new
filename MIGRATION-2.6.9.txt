VibePetCore 2.6.9

Scope: release-audit hotfix for Pet Source GUI, /pet context, and pet armor.

Player-visible changes:
- Legacy Source altar/entity clicks open the Source even when a pet core is held.
- /pet help now returns back to the personal pet screen instead of the Source hub.
- Pet armor progression is now monotonic:
  copper E1, iron E2, gold E3, diamond E4, netherite E5.
- Pet vault uses the strongest armor the pet is allowed to wear.
- Extra or too-high armor pieces are returned when the vault closes.
- Remaining visible "Pet Master" style wording in Source-facing text was replaced with "Source".

Admin notes:
- No Maven migration.
- No PetMaster data deletion.
- Existing player data is kept.
- After deploy, smoke-test Source opening, /pet, pet vault armor, quests, evolution, relog, and restart.
