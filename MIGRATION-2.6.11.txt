VibePetCore 2.6.11

Scope: quest reward and Source attempt clarity hotfix.

Player-visible changes:
- Quest turn-in now grants the full configured Pet Points reward.
- Anti-abuse point caps still apply to repeatable activity/kill rewards, but no longer truncate one-time quest turn-ins.
- Evolution quest titles now use the selected/active pet binding when deciding whether the quest is ready.
- Source box GUI now shows reserve attempts and says that openings can consume free cooldown, Pet Points, or reserve attempts.

Admin notes:
- No data migration is required.
- Existing quests already completed before this version are not retroactively paid again.
- Smoke-test one high-reward quest above the minute point cap and confirm full points are added.
